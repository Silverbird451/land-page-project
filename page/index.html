This website is about chickens

In this website our focus is chickens and the different breeds of them there are around this world, so if you are interested in chickens you will enjoy being here, that is why we recommend you to sign up.

Some breeds of chickens:

Isa Brown chickens: they are brown, weight in average of around 2kg and they are very docile.

Leghorn chickens: they are white with yellow legs, weight in average of around 3kg, and they are good at flying.

Speckled Sussex chicken: they are brown and speckled with white and their average weight is 4kg.

Barred Plymouth Rock chickens: has black and white barred feathers, average weight of 4kg and very sociable.
